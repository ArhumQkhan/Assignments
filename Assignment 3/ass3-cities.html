Islamabad

Islamabad, the capital city of Pakistan, is known for its modern architecture, orderly layout, and stunning natural beauty. Established in the 1960s, it is situated at the foothills of the Margalla Hills, offering a picturesque backdrop. The city is characterized by wide streets, green spaces, and a well-planned layout. Important government buildings, foreign embassies, and the iconic Faisal Mosque are key features. Islamabad's green spaces, including the Margalla Hills National Park, provide recreational opportunities. The city's vibrant culture and diverse culinary scene add to its charm, making it a unique and captivating destination.

Paris

Paris, the capital of France, is renowned for its elegance, history, and art. Iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and Louvre Museum define its skyline. Picturesque streets, charming neighborhoods, and world-class cuisine add to its allure, making it a top destination for travelers seeking culture and romance.
