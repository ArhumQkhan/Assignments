Machu Picchu

This ancient Inca city located high in the Andes Mountains is one of the most iconic archaeological sites in the world. Visitors can hike the Inca Trail to reach Machu Picchu or take a train from nearby Cusco.

Great Barrier Reef

Located off the coast of Queensland, the Great Barrier Reef is the world's largest coral reef system and is home to a diverse array of marine life. Visitors can snorkel, dive, or take a scenic flight over the reef.

Santorini

Known for its stunning sunsets, white-washed buildings, and blue-domed churches, Santorini is a popular destination for honeymooners and tourists looking for a romantic getaway. The island's volcanic beaches and crystal-clear waters add to its charm.

Banff National Park

Located in the Canadian Rockies, Banff National Park is known for its stunning mountain scenery, crystal-clear lakes, and abundant wildlife. Visitors can hike, bike, or ski in the park, depending on the season.

Serengeti National Park

Known for its vast plains and incredible wildlife, Serengeti National Park is a popular destination for safari enthusiasts. Visitors can see the Big Five (lion, elephant, buffalo, leopard, and rhinoceros) as well as the annual wildebeest migration.

Taj Mahal

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, the Taj Mahal is a stunning white marble mausoleum located in Agra. Built in the 17th century by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife, the Taj Mahal is considered one of the most beautiful buildings in the world.
